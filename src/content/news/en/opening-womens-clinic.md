The management of **Sarah Medical Center** is pleased to announce the relocation of the **English Clinic for Pregnancy, Childbirth & Women's Health** to its new home at Sarah Medical Center in Umm al-Fahm, in partnership with the **English Hospital in Nazareth**.

## Services Offered

- Routine and high-risk pregnancy monitoring
- Gestational diabetes management
- Childbirth preparation courses
- Lactation consulting
- Pregnancy exercise workshops
- Nutritional guidance and emotional support

## Medical Team

- **Dr. Mona Egbariah** — High-risk pregnancy specialist
- **Dr. Nazik Kareem** — Gynecologist
- **Midwife Iman Daher** — Certified midwife & lactation consultant

## Schedule

The clinic welcomes women on **Thursdays from 12:30 to 15:30**, with all services provided **free of charge** for all women, regardless of health fund.

For inquiries and appointments, please call **04-884-8080**.
